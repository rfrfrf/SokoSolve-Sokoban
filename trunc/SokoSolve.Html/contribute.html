SokoSolve | Contributions

SokoSolve is far from a finished product, as such there are many contributions needed. If you have the time and inclination, any help would be appreciated, and will be fully adknowledged. Contact bitwacker@users.sourceforge.net

Puzzles

SokoSolve needs new and interresting puzzle libraries that can be added to the distribution. General discussion for puzzles should take place on the discussion forums, along with with puzzle submissions.

Graphics

New skins, logos, buttons, backgrounds.

Sounds & Music

SokoSolve needs a rich array of sound effects and background music.

  • .MP3 highly compresses as looping background music
  • .WAV sound effects for steps, pushes, goal pushes, puzzle completion, etc.

Internationalisation

It would be great to get translations in place for other languages.

Testers

A group of testers is needed to check the release and suggest improvements.
